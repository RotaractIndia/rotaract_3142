Drop of Hope

Blood Donation Initiative,

Drop of Hope is an initiative of Rotaract District 3142 aimed at collecting blood by organizing blood donation camps throughout the Rotaract District 3142. Over 26+ locations had blood donation camps put up over the 4 zones of our district, with a huge increase in the year 2018-19, 1993 bottles of blood were collected by R.I.D. 3142. ‘Tears of a mother cannot save a Child, but one's blood can!’ Thereby, the blood collected in this initiative is used to help government / charitable trust blood banks. We were able to spread awareness on blood stem cell research and had over 1000+ generous people step forward and register themselves as blood stem cell donors. The fight against Thalassemia is real and together, Rotaractors hope as a city we are one step closer in making India Thalassemia free.
